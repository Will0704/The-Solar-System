Definition

-To know exactly what we mean when we sa "solar system" it is important to break it down into
the two words that comprise the expression.Firstly, the term "solar" is simply a word that means
"of the Sun." Secondly, a system is just a collection of objects that interact to form a whole.
Thus, putting the two words together provides the following definition:

"The solar system is a group of objects that interact with one another, the fundamental
interaction for each object being the it has with the Sun."
